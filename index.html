History

PRCD, or Professional Remote Control Drifters, founded in Manila in 2006, started with 21 members forming a committee to manage events and competitions, despite RC drifting already developing in the city since 2004.

Enthusiasts used improvised drift tires made from 2-inch PVC and ABS pipes initially. Later, major RC manufacturers like Yokomo and Tamiya introduced specialized drift tires and chassis, fueling the sport's growth.

In 2009, PRCD expanded with subgroups across the Philippines, gaining recognition through local and international events. In 2023, they'll represent the Philippines in the Thailand Drift Championship (TDC) in Jakarta, a significant milestone for the group and Philippine RC drifting.
